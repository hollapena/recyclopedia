#About Me

Hey, There!

I'm just your friendly neighborhood web developer that got fed up with the recycling system in her town. So I decided to do something about it!
Ok, actually this idea was hatched over a game night with some friends. It all started as a discussion...ahem*debate about whether you could recycle paper towels or not. That led to discussions about how many full batches of recycling are thrown out because of just a few wrongly recycled pieces. Well, I thought it might help to have a place to go for some of those more questionable items, and for general info about the three R's!
That's all I have for now, but feel free to stop by the contribution page, send an idea or just shoot over an e-mail...until next time neighbors, Stay Smart!
